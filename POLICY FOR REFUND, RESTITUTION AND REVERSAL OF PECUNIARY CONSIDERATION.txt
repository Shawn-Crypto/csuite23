\f0\b\fs36 \cf0 \strokec2 ARTICLE II: ELIGIBILITY MATRIX FOR REFUND CONSIDERATION\

\fs24 \cf0 2.1 Fundamental Prerequisites for Refund Eligibility
\f1\b0 \
No Subscriber shall be entitled to claim, demand, or receive any refund, restitution, or reversal of pecuniary consideration unless and until such Subscriber has satisfied each and every one of the following cumulative conditions precedent, the satisfaction of which shall be determined solely at the discretion of the Company:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  
\f0\b Extended Assessment Compliance
\f1\b0 : The Subscriber shall have completed the entire duration of the Mandatory Assessment Period of ninety-six (96) consecutive hours without interruption, suspension, or premature termination, and shall submit any application for refund consideration only during the period commencing on the fifth (5th) day following the date of purchase and ending on the tenth (10th) day following such date of purchase, both days inclusive;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  
\f0\b Comprehensive Educational Engagement
\f1\b0 : The Subscriber shall have completed, to the entire satisfaction of the Company, all Core Module Completion Requirements as evidenced by the Company's tracking and monitoring systems, including achievement of minimum seventy percent (70%) scores on all assessments;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Enhanced Engagement Verification
\f1\b0 : The Subscriber shall have satisfied the Minimum Engagement Threshold of twelve (12) hours as recorded and verified by the Company's technological infrastructure;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  
\f0\b Procedural Compliance Demonstration
\f1\b0 : The Subscriber shall have complied with all procedural requirements set forth in Article IV hereof;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  
\f0\b Absence of Disqualifying Events
\f1\b0 : No Disqualifying Events shall have occurred in relation to the Subscriber or the Subscriber's use of the Educational Services;\

\f0\b (f)
\f1\b0  
\f0\b Investment Performance Disclaimer Acknowledgment
\f1\b0 : The Subscriber must demonstrate understanding that investment education does not guarantee market performance and that refund requests based on market losses or investment outcomes are categorically excluded.\

\f0\b 2.2 Substantive Grounds for Refund Consideration
\f1\b0 \
Even upon satisfaction of all conditions precedent set forth in sub-clause 2.1 hereof, refund consideration shall be available solely upon demonstration by the Subscriber of Qualifying Deficiency, supported by:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Independent financial education expert report validating alleged deficiencies in course methodology;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Comparative analysis with recognized industry-standard financial education curricula;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Documentary evidence of material misrepresentations in course content not attributable to market conditions or regulatory changes;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Technical audit report from certified IT professionals regarding platform functionality deficiencies.\

\f0\b 2.3 Absolute Exclusions from Refund Eligibility
\f1\b0 \
Refund consideration shall be categorically unavailable in the following circumstances:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Investment losses or poor market performance subsequent to course completion;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Changes in market conditions, regulatory environment, or economic circumstances;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Subscriber's failure to implement course teachings or investment strategies;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Subjective dissatisfaction with course presentation style or teaching methodology;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  Technical issues attributable to Subscriber's equipment, internet connectivity, or device limitations;\

\f0\b (f)
\f1\b0  Force majeure events affecting course delivery or market conditions.\

\f0\b\fs36 \cf0 \strokec2 ARTICLE III: ABSOLUTE DISQUALIFICATION CRITERIA\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa240\partightenfactor0

\fs24 \cf0 3.1 Temporal Disqualification Events
\f1\b0 \
The following temporal events shall constitute Disqualifying Events rendering any Subscriber absolutely and irrevocably ineligible for refund consideration:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Submission of refund application prior to completion of the Mandatory Assessment Period of ninety-six (96) hours;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Submission of refund application subsequent to the expiration of ten (10) calendar days from the date of purchase;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Failure to complete the Mandatory Assessment Period within the prescribed timeframe without acceptable justification;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Any interruption, suspension, or premature termination of the Mandatory Assessment Period by the Subscriber for reasons not constituting force majeure.\

\f0\b 3.2 Engagement-Based Disqualification Events
\f1\b0 \
The following engagement-related events shall constitute Disqualifying Events:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Failure to complete any component of the Core Module Completion Requirements;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Achievement of assessment scores below the minimum seventy percent (70%) threshold;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Failure to satisfy the Minimum Engagement Threshold of twelve (12) hours;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Any attempt to circumvent, bypass, or manipulate the Company's tracking and monitoring systems;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  Completion of educational modules beyond the core modules;\

\f0\b (f)
\f1\b0  Utilization of course materials for actual investment decisions or trading activities.\

\f0\b 3.3 Usage-Based Disqualification Events
\f1\b0 \
The following usage-related events shall constitute Disqualifying Events:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Downloading, saving, copying, or otherwise obtaining offline access to any Proprietary Materials;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Sharing, distributing, or providing access to the Educational Services to any third party;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Attempting to reverse engineer, decompile, or extract any Proprietary Materials or investment methodologies;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Commercial use of course content, strategies, or methodologies;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  Creation of competing educational products using Company's intellectual property;\

\f0\b (f)
\f1\b0  Violation of any intellectual property rights or confidentiality obligations.\

\f0\b 3.4 Market-Related Disqualification Events
\f1\b0 \
The following market-related events shall constitute Disqualifying Events:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  Making investment decisions based on course content during the assessment period;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  Attributing investment losses to course inadequacies;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  Expecting guaranteed investment returns or market performance;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  Failure to acknowledge inherent market risks and volatility;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  Unrealistic expectations regarding investment education outcomes.\
