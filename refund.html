Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 5th July 2025

We are confident in the value of "The Complete Indian Stock Investor." However, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines the terms for refunds and cancellations.

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee from the date of purchase, subject to the conditions below.

2. Conditions for Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a full refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • You must have completed less than 25% of the total course content. Your course progress is tracked automatically on our platform.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please send an email to investoreducation@lotuslion.in with the subject line "Refund Request." Please include your full name, registered email address, and the reason for your request.

4. Refund Processing

Once your request is received and approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days. The refund will be credited back to the original method of payment.

5. Non-Refundable Circumstances

A refund will not be provided in the following circumstances:

  • If the refund request is made more than 30 days after the date of purchase.
  • If you have completed 25% or more of the course content.
  • If your account has been suspended due to a violation of our Terms and Conditions.

6. Course Cancellation

As this is a self-paced online course with lifetime access, there is no "cancellation" process once purchased. If you no longer wish to continue, you may simply stop accessing the course. If you wish to have your account and data deleted, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

7. Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties accessing the course, please contact our support team at investoreducation@lotuslion.in. We will make every effort to resolve the issue promptly. Technical issues do not automatically qualify for a refund but will be addressed on a case-by-case basis if they prevent you from accessing the course content.
