\fs24 \cf0 1.1 Statutory and Regulatory Definitions
\f1\b0 \
For the purposes of this Policy, and in accordance with applicable data protection legislation, the following expressions shall bear the meanings assigned to them under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and related regulations:\

\f0\b (a)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Personal Data
\f1\b0 " shall mean any data about an individual who is identifiable by or in relation to such data, including but not limited to name, contact information, demographic details, financial information, investment preferences, educational records, behavioral data, device identifiers, biometric information, and any other information that directly or indirectly identifies a natural person;\

\f0\b (b)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Sensitive Personal Data
\f1\b0 " shall mean personal data comprising financial information, investment portfolios, trading records, bank account details, health records, official identifiers, biometric data, genetic data, religious or political beliefs, or any other categories designated as sensitive under applicable law;\

\f0\b (c)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Data Principal
\f1\b0 " shall mean the individual to whom personal data relates, including prospective users, registered users, subscribers, course participants, investors, and any other natural persons whose data is processed by the Company;\

\f0\b (d)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Data Fiduciary
\f1\b0 " shall mean the Company in its capacity as the entity that determines the purpose and means of processing personal data;\

\f0\b (e)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Data Processor
\f1\b0 " shall mean any entity that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Fiduciary, including third-party service providers, technology vendors, payment processors, and business associates;\

\f0\b (f)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Processing
\f1\b0 " shall mean any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, dissemination, restriction, erasure, or destruction;\

\f0\b (g)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Consent
\f1\b0 " shall mean any freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the Data Principal's agreement to the processing of personal data relating to them, as evidenced through affirmative action or clear opt-in mechanisms;\

\f0\b (h)
\f1\b0  "
\f0\b Cross-Border Data Transfer
\f1\b0 " shall mean transfer of personal data from India to any country outside India or to any international organization, whether through direct transmission, cloud storage, or third-party service provider arrangements.\

\f0\b (a)
